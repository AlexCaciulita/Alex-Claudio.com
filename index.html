The Experience

A calm, considered process
from first letter to archive.

No pressure, no upsells. Four quiet chapters that begin the day you write and end with photographs in your hands.

01

A first letter

Tell me a little about your day: venue, vibe, the people who make it yours. We'll exchange notes, and I'll send my full guide.

02

The planning call

A 30-minute call to shape the timeline, light, and any moments you want held close. We cover first looks, golden hour, and anything private.

03

The wedding day

I arrive early, move quietly, and make pictures. Gentle direction when it helps, invisible when it matters. No forced poses, no fuss.

04

Your archive

Four weeks later: a hand-edited gallery, a private film, and an heirloom folio option. Images you can hang in your home and pass down.
