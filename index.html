About Me

With over 10 years of experience in documentary wedding photography, I've had the privilege of capturing hundreds of love stories across the country and around the world. My approach is rooted in photojournalism. I blend into the background to capture authentic moments while also creating beautifully composed portraits that showcase your unique connection.

I believe every couple deserves to have their wedding day documented with artistry and care. Whether you're planning an intimate elopement at Volunteer Park, a downtown rooftop party, or a grand celebration in Bellevue or Snoqualmie, I'm here to tell your story through images that will be treasured for generations.

Craft your legacy

I’ll travel across Seattle, the Eastside, and the greater Puget Sound — ferries, forests, rooftops, and wineries included. Destination weddings? Absolutely. Tell me where and we’ll make it happen.

Rain or shine, I bring clear umbrellas, backup plans, and calm energy so the day feels effortless.

Discover the unseen

Documentary-first with editorial polish. I blend into the moment, then guide when you need it so the images feel cinematic and true to you.

Expect location scouting, timeline support, and delivery built for sharing with family, friends, and vendors.
