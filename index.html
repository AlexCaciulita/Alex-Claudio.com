About Alex Claudio

With over 14 years of experience in documentary wedding photography, I've had the privilege of capturing hundreds of love stories across the country and around the world. My approach is rooted in photojournalism.I blend into the background to capture authentic moments while also creating beautifully composed portraits that showcase your unique connection.

I believe every couple deserves to have their wedding day documented with artistry and care. Whether you're planning an intimate elopement or a grand celebration, I'm here to tell your story through images that will be treasured for generations.
