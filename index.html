About Me

With over 14 years of experience in documentary wedding photography, I've had the privilege of capturing hundreds of love stories across the country and around the world. My approach is rooted in photojournalism. I blend into the background to capture authentic moments while also creating beautifully composed portraits that showcase your unique connection.

I believe every couple deserves to have their wedding day documented with artistry and care. Whether you're planning an intimate elopement at Volunteer Park, a downtown rooftop party, or a grand celebration in Bellevue or Snoqualmie, I'm here to tell your story through images that will be treasured for generations.

FAQs

Seattle wedding photography essentials

Answers to common questions so you know exactly what working together looks like.

How far do you travel?

I regularly photograph weddings across Seattle, the Eastside, and the greater Puget Sound. I also travel for destination weddings and elopements—just share the details and I’ll build a custom plan.

Do you shoot in the rain?

Absolutely. This is Seattle—rain plans are part of the process. I scout covered locations, bring clear umbrellas, and adjust timelines to keep things relaxed and photo-friendly.

Can you help with locations?

Yes. I recommend Seattle and Eastside engagement spots, ferry rides, and PNW trails that fit your vibe, and I coordinate with venues to keep portraits smooth and efficient.

What is your style?

Documentary-first with editorial polish. You’ll get candid, emotional images plus guided portraits that feel natural and true to you.
