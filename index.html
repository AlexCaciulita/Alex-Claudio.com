Meet the Photographer

About Alex | Seattle Wedding Photographer

For over a decade, I've had the incredible privilege of documenting love stories across the Pacific Northwest. Based in Seattle, I specialize in documentary-style wedding photography with editorial polish—capturing authentic moments as they unfold while guiding you when you need it.

My approach is simple: I believe your wedding day should feel like YOUR day, not a photoshoot. I blend into the background to capture genuine emotions, candid laughter, and those fleeting moments you might otherwise miss. When it's time for portraits, I provide gentle direction to create timeless, artful images that feel natural and authentic.

What Makes My Style Unique

  • Documentary: I capture real moments as they happen—candid emotions, genuine laughter, stolen glances, and authentic interactions.
  • Editorial: During portraits and key moments, I provide gentle direction to create artful, magazine-worthy images with intentional composition and beautiful light.
  • The Result: A complete story of your day with a balance of photojournalistic authenticity and editorial artistry.

At Home in the Pacific Northwest

Living and working in Seattle means I know this region intimately. From the dramatic waterfront at Golden Gardens to the urban elegance of downtown venues, from the lush forests of the Eastside to the stunning mountain backdrops—I know where to find the best light, the hidden photo spots, and how to make the most of every location.

I also know Seattle weather. Rain doesn't phase me—I bring clear umbrellas, embrace the moody atmosphere, and always have backup plans. Some of my favorite images have been captured on misty, romantic Seattle days.
